About Us

Liya Baby Photography Studio

Liya Baby Photography Studio, nestled in the heart of Pallikaranai, Chennai, is a premier destination for maternity, newborn, and baby photography. Our spacious, fully air-conditioned studio is thoughtfully equipped with a wide variety of costumes, props, and themed setups to make every photoshoot a magical and comfortable experience for the entire family. With a stellar rating of 4.8 out of 5 from over 239 happy families, we take pride in delivering photographs that are not just images, but cherished lifelong memories.

Our passionate team of photographers, led by dedicated professionals like Priyanka and Priya, go above and beyond to ensure every client feels relaxed, comfortable, and truly special during their session. From the very first maternity shoot to newborn portraits and toddler milestones, Liya Studio becomes your trusted partner through every beautiful chapter of parenthood. Cleanliness, warmth, and an eye for detail define everything we do — because your little one deserves nothing but the best.
